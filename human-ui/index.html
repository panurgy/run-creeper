Run Creeper

The fastest way to search for running results!
Every year, there are lots of great racing events available to participants in a wide range of abilities. The results from those races are organized with easily searchable human-friendly interfaces.
This is an interface to those interfaces When your web browser submits a search request, the search parameters are forwarded onto lots of other sites, and all of the results are aggregated back to your browser.
